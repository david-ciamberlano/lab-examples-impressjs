Identity Gateway

Identity Governance

  • Helps you automate account and entitlement provisioning and access request workflows

Why an Identity Platform?

  • An Identity Platform brings all these capabilities together into a single platform

  • Improved user experience - components work together as a whole
  • Reduces costs - no need to integrate the different components
  • More Secure - no gaps between solutions
